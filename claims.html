The Process

How to File a Claim in 4 Steps

Our fully digital claims process means no paperwork, no branch visits, and no delays.

01

Select Your Policy

Log into the Laxman Financial Freedom portal and select the policy under which you want to raise a claim.

02

Upload Documents

Submit required documents digitally — medical bills, ID proof, or incident reports. All uploaded securely online.

03

Expert Verification

Our claims team reviews your documents with full transparency. You receive SMS and email status updates throughout.

04

Direct Settlement

Your claim amount is credited directly to your bank account within 3 working days. Guaranteed, every single time.

Documents Required

What You'll Need to Submit

Keep these documents ready to make your claim process as smooth as possible.

🪪

Identity Proof

Aadhaar card, PAN card, or passport. Any government-issued photo ID is accepted.

📋

Policy Documents

Your original policy number and policy certificate. Available in your online account.

🏥

Medical Records

Hospital bills, discharge summary, prescription, and investigation reports for health claims.

🚗

FIR / Police Report

Required for motor theft, accident, and home burglary claims. Lodge with nearest police station.

🏦

Bank Account Details

Cancelled cheque or bank statement for direct settlement transfer to your account.

📸

Photo Evidence

Photographs of damage (vehicle, property, etc.) taken at the time of incident if available.
