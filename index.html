Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our insurance products and services.

Laxman Financial Freedom maintains an impressive 99% claim settlement ratio, one of the highest in the industry. This means we settle 99 out of every 100 claims we receive. Our transparent process and customer-first approach ensure genuine claims are processed quickly and fairly.

Our average claim settlement time is just 3 working days. For health insurance claims with cashless facilities, we can approve within 24-48 hours. Life insurance claims with complete documentation are settled within 3-5 days. We also offer expedited processing for emergency cases.

Yes! We offer seamless policy portability. You can transfer your existing life, health, car, or home insurance policy from any IRDAI-registered insurer to Laxman Financial Freedom. You'll retain benefits like no-claim bonus and waiting period credits while getting better coverage and service.

Documents vary by policy type:

  • Life Insurance: Death certificate, policy document, claim form, ID proof
  • Health Insurance: Medical reports, bills, discharge summary, claim form
  • Car Insurance: FIR report, driving license, RC book, photos of damage
  • Home Insurance: Incident report, valuation reports, repair estimates

Yes, we provide cashless hospitalization at 10,000+ network hospitals across India. Simply present your Laxman Health Card at any network hospital, and we'll settle bills directly with the hospital up to your policy limit. No upfront payment required for covered treatments.

Renewal is completely digital and takes just 3 minutes:

  1. Log into your account on our website or app
  2. Go to 'My Policies' and select the policy to renew
  3. Review coverage details and make any changes
  4. Pay securely via any payment method
  5. Receive instant policy confirmation

We send renewal reminders 30, 15, and 3 days before expiry.

Absolutely! All our policies are fully customizable. You can adjust coverage limits, add riders (like critical illness cover, accidental death benefit), choose deductibles, and select payment frequency. Our insurance advisors help you create a personalized plan that fits your exact needs and budget.

We offer multiple payment options for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • Net Banking (all major banks)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • EMI options on credit cards
  • Auto-debit mandate for renewals
  • Cheque/DD at our branches
